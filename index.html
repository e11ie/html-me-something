Oh! The Places I've Been!

Badlands, SD

Best stop to take some pictures when you drive by! This view is especially beautiful at sunset.

Mt Rushmore, SD

If you haven't been it definately a historical landmark to put on the Bucket List.

Yellowstone National Park, CO

This is a huge park with lots and lots of things to do: the iconic waterfall in the canyon, moose (and bears!), bubbling mud, geysers, and deeply colorful hot springs. Outdoor-sy people this is for you!

Fort Henry, MD

Another historial landmark for the books!

Hannibal, MO

This is the place where Mark Twain grew up. Come and take a tour through his childhood home or eat on a steamboat!

Elephant Rock State Park, MO

As a kid I absolutely loved coming here to play and explore. Who doesn't like to climb?

Silver Dollar City, MO

Did you know that the Marvel Cave tours came first and the park with the attracions, rides and shows sprung up after?

Vancouver, Canada

I love summer in Canada! The weather is not nearly so hot and the gardens are beautiful

Niagra Falls, Canada

Truely makes me feel tiny in regard to the awesome power of nature.
